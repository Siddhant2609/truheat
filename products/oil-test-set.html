Overview

Our Oil Test Sets are ideal for speedy and accurate testing of dielectric strength of transformer and circuit breaker oils as per IS:6792-1972.

Output Range

Continuously variable output from 0–50KV, 0–60KV, 0–75KV, 0–100KV, or 0–120KV. Sets are available in manual or motor operated variants.

Controls & Features

  • Auto-Transformer — Continuously variable for smooth voltage variation
  • Automatic Tripping — Protection mechanism for H.T. transformer
  • KV Voltmeter — Calibrated in KV on L.T. side for accurate readings
  • Zero-Start Interlock — Safety interlocking device prevents accidental operation
  • Memory Device — Displays breakdown voltage after trip for recording

Test Vessel

Fitted with two brass spheres (Ø 12.7mm) placed at 2.5mm gap distance, adjustable, made from non-absorbent insulating material. The vessel design ensures accurate and repeatable test results conforming to Indian Standards.

Available Configurations

  • Manual Operation — Hand-operated voltage control
  • Motor Operated — Motorized voltage control for precision
  • Portable Models — Field-ready units for on-site testing
  • Bench-Top Models — Laboratory installation
