Transformer Oil Filter — Multi Stage

Double or three stage vacuum filter systems for achieving the highest standards of oil purity. Ideal when superior dissolved gas removal and ultra-low moisture content is critical.

Overview

For cases where a higher state of vacuum is required to maintain low value of dissolved gases and moisture, we offer double or three stage vacuum filter systems.

The added advantage includes improving quality of filtered oil to a higher level under single pass at approximately 60°C.

Special Features

  • Alarm Annunciators — Real-time alerts for process monitoring
  • Moisture Content Meter — Continuous moisture level monitoring
  • Gas Content Meter — Dissolved gas measurement
  • Digital Electronic Measuring Instruments — Precision readings
  • Oil Flow Meter — Flow rate monitoring and control
  • Transformer Evacuation System — Complete vacuum capability

Ionic Reaction Column

For cases requiring decreased acid content, an ionic reaction column is provided complete with high grade neutralizing chemicals and stainless steel wire mesh supporting media.

Performance Comparison

Multi-stage systems deliver dramatically better results than single-stage under the same conditions:

  • Dielectric Strength — Achieves 75–80 KV (vs 60 KV single stage)
  • Moisture Content — Reduces to 2 ppm (vs 20 ppm single stage)
  • Suspended Impurities — Down to 2 micron (vs 10 micron)
  • Gas Content — Reduces to 0.05% (vs 5% single stage)
