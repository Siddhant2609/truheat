About TRUHEAT

Engineering Excellence in Oil Purification

TRUHEAT is a leading manufacturer of transformer oil filtering machines and high voltage testing equipment. With over five decades of experience, our team of R&D experts have been consistently successful in eliminating flaws and delivering maximum value to our esteemed customers.

Our product range spans from compact single-stage oil purifiers to advanced multi-stage vacuum filtration systems, along with a complete suite of electrical testing instruments used by power utilities, transformer manufacturers, and service companies across India.
